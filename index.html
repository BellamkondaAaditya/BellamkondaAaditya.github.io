Using ML to estimate Area Burned by a wildfire
In January of 2021, bush fires ravaged the land of Australia, killing an estimated 3 billion wild animals in the process. As salient features data is available and readily accessible, I utilized this data for predicting the area burned as a means to be better equipped at dealing with wildfires.
